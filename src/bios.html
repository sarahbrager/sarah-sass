Meet The Band

George H.

George is an Austin-native and has been playing bass for 50 years!

Paul M.

Paul moved from Phoenix to Austin in 2016 to join the band. He slays on keys, guitar and vocals.

Ringo S.

Ringo, the band's drummer, motivated Paul and George to join the trio. The three started the band in 2016 in honor of their late college buddy, Harvey Dale.
